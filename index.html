UNITED SIKHS is a UN-affiliated international humanitarian NGO founded in 1999. For over 25 years, we've provided disaster relief, legal aid, education, and community development across North America, Asia, Africa, Australia, and Europe. We hold an 84% rating on Charity Navigator. Our work is guided by the Sikh principle of seva (selfless service) - recognizing the human race as one.
