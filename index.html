Panjab's Worst Floods in Four Decades

In August 2025, relentless monsoon rains and dam releases triggered catastrophic flooding across Panjab's border districts. Entire communities were cut off. Families climbed to rooftops. Children watched their homes wash away.

UNITED SIKHS deployed rescue boats within 24 hours, operating through the night to save lives. We've rescued over 2,000 people, served thousands of hot meals daily, and committed to rebuilding 11 villages from the ground up.

But the work isn't finished.

Winter is here. Families need permanent homes. Children need schools. Farmers need seeds. Your year-end gift makes this possible.

Source: 2025 Panjab, India floods, Wikipedia, Panjab Floods Secondary Data Analysis Report (September 2025)
