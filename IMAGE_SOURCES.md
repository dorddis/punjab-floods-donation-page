## Legal Notes

### Fair Use Justification
All news media images are used under fair use doctrine for:
1. **Non-commercial purpose**: Non-profit disaster relief campaign
2. **Transformative use**: Educational/awareness for humanitarian cause
3. **Minimal impact**: Does not compete with original news sources
4. **Public interest**: Disaster relief and humanitarian aid

### Attribution Policy
- All images are properly attributed to original sources
- News organizations are credited in image captions
- Links to original sources maintained for transparency
- Images used solely for non-profit disaster relief awareness

### Client-Provided Assets
Images from United Sikhs/Global Sikhs are:
- Authorized for use by client
- Part of official campaign materials
- Properly licensed for this purpose

---

## Recommendations for Production

### Before Public Launch:
1. **Get explicit permission** from news outlets for image use
2. **Replace with client-owned photos** if available
3. **Add visible attribution** on images if required
4. **Consider Getty Images license** for stock flood photos
5. **Document all permissions** in writing

### Alternative Approach:
- Use only United Sikhs-owned photos
- Commission photographer for disaster documentation
- Purchase rights to news agency photos
- Use Creative Commons licensed images
